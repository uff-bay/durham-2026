RSVP to plant trees with us!

Come plant trees at your school! Urban Forest Friends will be working with the City of Fremont and FUSD to plant thirty-four (34) going into Durham Elementary School.

This is a great opportunity for you and your child to learn how to properly plant trees, help your community, and fight climate change.

Your child must be in 3rd, 4th or 5th grade to volunteer.

Limit is one child per parent.

Important
